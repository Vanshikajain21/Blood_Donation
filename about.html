About US

Our aim is to contribute to the well-being of our community by organizing a voluntary blood donation drive. The primary goal is to collect a substantial amount of blood from willing donors to support local hospitals and save lives.

Our Blood Donation Camps are being organized regularly especially in rural areas & educational institutions to create awareness among children & masses in rural areas and to promote voluntary blood donation. Donors, Motivators & Camp Organizers from villages have been properly trained for the purpose. The purpose of blood donation camp to select a suitable donor whose blood will be safe to the recipient and who himself shall not in any way be harmed by blood donation.
